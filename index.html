The problem

AI agents hallucinate because
the knowledge layer is missing.

The teams building AI in finance, healthcare, and regulated industries are moving fast — but the domain knowledge underneath is informal, inconsistent, or absent. Terms like "non-performing loan" or "beneficial owner" mean specific, legally significant things. A model that doesn't know that is a liability.

I close that gap with formal ontologies, governed taxonomies, and semantic data models that give AI systems the structured domain knowledge they need to reason reliably — not just generate plausibly.
