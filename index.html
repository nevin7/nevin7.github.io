Why This Matters

AI agents hallucinate because they lack well-structured domain knowledge.

The teams building AI products in finance, healthcare, and regulated industries are moving fast — but the knowledge layer underneath is often informal, inconsistent, or missing entirely. Terms mean different things across systems. Business logic isn't captured anywhere a model can use it.

That's the gap I close. I design the ontologies, taxonomies, and semantic models that give AI systems the formal, governed domain knowledge they need to be reliable — not just capable.

How I Work

Formal structure. Practical outcomes.

01

Discover & Model

SME workshops and stakeholder discovery sessions to surface implicit domain knowledge, then model it formally in OWL, SKOS, or RDF with clear governance principles.

02

Align & Govern

Map to authoritative standards — FIBO for finance, HL7 for health, government data models — and define the metadata governance processes that keep it accurate over time.

03

Connect & Enable

Wire the knowledge layer into your data catalog, RAG pipeline, or agent system. Document it so data engineers and AI practitioners can actually use it.
